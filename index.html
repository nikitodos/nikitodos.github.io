Welcome to the Nikitodom! The name of this website is ispired by the official Windows 2000 slogan created by Microsoft at its launch.

Some years ago I imagined how wonderful it would be to make some device which had the possibility of ascending in the atmosphere, and than even could go to Mars. I was a different boy then, but I am still looking at the stars, and looking for the several ways I could reach them.

Maybe it's my past's fault if Robert Goddard is one of my personal heroes. Once, he said: Every vision is a joke until the first man accomplishes it; once realized, it becomes commonplace. The image you can see here is a collage of my first concepts, and through this website I hope you will discover interesting ideas that may fit good with this definition.
