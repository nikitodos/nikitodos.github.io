Learn, Train, Fly

Design, build and test parts of a Moth sailing boat to develop your engineering capabilities, manage social networks, draw up a business plan to master your economic and legal skills. Put into practice your theoretical notions by building flying boats!

With the goal of partecipating to the SuMoth Challenge, a new boat is presented every two years. New designs and eco-sustainable ideas compete with the ideas of students coming from all over the world.

SuMoth in few words

The Foiling SuMoth Challenge is a student competition with the ultimate objective to design, manufacture and sail the most sustainable and efficient foiling international moths. To accomplish this, we create a community framework where the future professionals have the chance to try and validate new innovative ideas before getting into the industry.

This is linked to the Goals for Sustainable Development (GSD) with a focus on the world of sailing and the marine environment. Climate action, Quality Education and Gender equality, are some of the goals from the GSD that we focus on. Promoting the “3R” principle of REduce, REuse, REcycle, the open access to all information, results and masterclasses In 2022, the SuMoth fleet was 100% skipped by females.
