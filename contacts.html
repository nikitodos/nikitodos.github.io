Something about me.

My name is Giovanni D'Aloisio. According to 16 Personalities I am an ENTJ-T. I am a Physicist and a student in Engineering. I am interested in space, naval architecture and astronomy, and I am the Team Leader of UniPV Sailing Team since September 2023.

In 2023 I took part, as an intern at BSRC, to a research project in the Search of ExtraTerrestrial Intelligence, under the supervision of the University of California Berkeley. At the Sardinia Radio Telescope in Cagliari, Italy, as an "alien hunter", I developed algorithms to implement KLT on datasets acquired in C and K band. The main targets on which the test was undertaken were the galactic nuclei and a puffy and hot Neptune-sized planet around the habitable zone of its star.

Moreover, I am the lecturer for two introductory courses in LᴬTᴇX and web design since 2022. In my free time, I enjoy writing music and lyrics. Last but not least, in the past, I have been a Cadet Engineer on board Amerigo Vespucci and intern in an electrical power plant. Those experiences gave me most of the inspiration for the ideas and prototypes you can see in this website.
