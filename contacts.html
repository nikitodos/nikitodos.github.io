Insights Into My Journey

Hello, I'm Giovanni D'Aloisio. According to 16 Personalities, I am an ENTJ-T. As a Physicist pursuing Space Engineering, I strive to see what everyone has seen and think what no one has thought. Fueled by an unrelenting passion for space exploration and innovation, I believe in dreaming big and breaking boundaries to reshape our knowledge.

In 2023, I embarked on an internship at BSRC, contributing to a research endeavor in the Search for Extraterrestrial Intelligence (SETI) under the auspices of the University of California, Berkeley. At the Sardinia Radio Telescope in Cagliari, Italy, I delved into the realm of "alien hunting," developing algorithms to apply the Karhunen–Loève transform (KLT) on datasets captured in C and K band frequencies. My focus laid on galactic nuclei and a Neptune-sized exoplanet situated within the habitable zone of its star. I continue to contribute to this project within the realm of Breakthrough Listen.

From September 2023 to July 2024, I served as Team Leader for the UniPV Sailing Team, and I continue to hold the position of Chief Designer, with my term ad interim set to conclude in July 2025. Since 2022, I have also been an instructor for introductory courses in LᴬTᴇX and web design. In my free time, I enjoy composing music and writing lyrics. I have had the privilege of serving as a Cadet Engineer aboard the Amerigo Vespucci and contributing to operations at an electrical power plant. These diverse experiences have deeply shaped the innovative ideas and prototypes I share on this platform.
