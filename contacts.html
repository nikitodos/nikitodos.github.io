Something about me.

Long live and prosper! My name is Giovanni D'Aloisio, on Wikimedia Project Dnepr740 (last update: 2019). According to 16 Peronalities I am an ENTJ-T. I am a Physicist and a Student in Space Communication and Sensing. I am interested in Space and Naval Architecture, and since September 2023, I am the Team Leader and Chief Designer of UniPV Sailing Team.

In 2023 I took part, as an Intern at BSRC, to a research project about the Search of ExtraTerrestrial Intelligence. At the Sardinia Radio Telescope in Cagliari, Italy, as an "alien hunter", I developed algorithms to implement KLT on datasets acquired in C and K band. The main targets on which the test were undertaken were the galactic nuclei and a puffy and hot Neptune-sized planet around the habitable zone of its star. Actually, I'm working on new developments of that project.

Moreover, I am the lecturer for two introductory courses in LᴬTᴇX and web design since 2022. In my free time, I enjoy writing music and lyrics.

In the past, I have been a Cadet Engineer on board Amerigo Vespucci and intern in an electrical power plant.
