Insights Into My Journey

I'm Giovanni D'Aloisio. According to 16 Personalities, I identify as an ENTJ-T. By profession, I'm a Physicist pursuing studies in Engineering, driven by a passion for space, naval architecture, and astronomy. Since September 2023, I've held the role of Team Leader and Chief Designer at UniPV Sailing Team.

In 2023, I embarked on an internship at BSRC, contributing to a research endeavor in the Search for Extraterrestrial Intelligence (SETI) under the auspices of the University of California, Berkeley. At the Sardinia Radio Telescope in Cagliari, Italy, I delved into the realm of "alien hunting," developing algorithms to apply the Karhunen–Loève transform (KLT) on datasets captured in C and K band frequencies. My focus laid on galactic nuclei and a Neptune-sized exoplanet situated within the habitable zone of its star. I continue to contribute to this project within the realm of Breakthrough Listen.

Furthermore, since 2022, I've been an instructor for introductory courses in LᴬTᴇX and web design. In my leisure time, I find solace in composing music and lyrics. Notably, I've served as a Cadet Engineer aboard Amerigo Vespucci and interned at an electrical power plant. These diverse experiences have significantly influenced the ideas and prototypes showcased on this platform.
